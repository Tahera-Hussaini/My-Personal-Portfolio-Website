About Me

I’m Tahera Hussaini based in Baghlan province, Afghanistan, with a strong interest in digital creativity and modern web technologies. Besides transitioning into the technology field, I built a diverse professional background in the public sector, INGOs, and UN. I have contributed to humanitarian and public health programs in various roles, including Community Health Worker (CHW), Supervisor, Coordinator & Monitor in NIDs, Trainer, Social Mobilizer, First Responder (SART), and Safeguarding Policy focal point (Investigator) in Aga Khan Agency for Habitat (AKAH) across Baghlan, Balkh and Bamiyan provinces.

I have contributed to several key projects, including WASEP, CERT, SART, and Echo, gaining hands-on experiences in program coordination, monitoring, and community outreach. Now I am advancing into web design and commitment to delivering meaningful digital solutions that make a positive social impact.
